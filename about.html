Our Story

Fair & Glow Beauty Salon was founded by Mrs. Geeta Gupta with a vision to provide premium beauty services that enhance natural beauty while ensuring a luxurious experience for every client.

What started as a small beauty parlor has now grown into a full-service premium salon, known for its exceptional quality and personalized approach to beauty. Our journey has been defined by our commitment to excellence and our passion for helping our clients look and feel their best.

Today, Fair & Glow stands as a testament to Mrs. Gupta's dedication and expertise in the beauty industry. We continue to evolve, embracing new techniques and trends while staying true to our core values of quality, professionalism, and client satisfaction.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional beauty services that enhance our clients' natural beauty and boost their confidence, delivered by skilled professionals in a welcoming and luxurious environment.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier beauty destination that sets the standard for excellence in beauty services, continuously innovating while maintaining the highest levels of client satisfaction.

Meet Our Founder

Mrs. Geeta Gupta

Founder & Lead Beautician

With over 18 years of experience in the beauty industry, Mrs. Geeta Gupta has established herself as an expert in bridal makeup, skincare, and beauty treatments. Her passion for beauty and commitment to excellence has been the driving force behind Fair & Glow's success.

Mrs. Gupta holds certifications from prestigious beauty academies and regularly updates her skills to stay at the forefront of beauty trends and techniques. Her artistic approach to makeup and deep understanding of skin types has earned her a loyal clientele and recognition in the beauty community.

Under her leadership, Fair & Glow Beauty Salon has grown from a small parlor to a premium beauty destination, known for its high-quality services and customer-centric approach.
